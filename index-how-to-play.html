How to Play

  1. Two players: one is X and the other is O.
  2. X always goes first; O goes second.
  3. Players take turns clicking any empty box to place their symbol.
  4. The first player to get 3 of the same symbols in a straight line (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal) wins.
  5. Only the most recent 6 moves remain on the board at any time.
  6. When the move limit is reached, the oldest move turns red to indicate it will be removed next.
  7. As soon as a new move exceeds the limit, the red (oldest) box is removed automatically.
  8. Click the Reset Game button at any time to start a fresh match.
  9. Ready for a bigger challenge? Tap Try Tristrike to play the larger 5x5 version.
