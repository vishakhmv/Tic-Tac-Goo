How to Play

  1. This is a two-player game. One player plays with X and the other plays with O.
  2. The game always starts with X. O plays second.
  3. Players take turns clicking on an empty box to place their symbol.
  4. The first player to make three same symbols in a straight line (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal) wins the game.
  5. Only the last 6 moves stay on the board at any time.
  6. The oldest move turns red to show which block will be removed next.
  7. When a new move is made, the red (oldest) block is removed automatically from the board.
  8. You can click the Reset Game button anytime to start a new match.
  9. Feeling confident? Tap Try Tristrike to enjoy the bigger 5x5 version!
