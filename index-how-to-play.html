How to Play

  1. This is a two-player game. One player plays with X and the other plays with O.
  2. The game always begins with X. After that, O takes the next turn.
  3. Players take turns clicking any empty box to place their symbol.
  4. The first player to align three identical symbols in a straight line (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal) wins the match.
  5. Only the last 6 moves remain visible on the board at all times.
  6. When the move limit is reached, the oldest active move turns red to show that it will be removed next.
  7. When a new move is played after this, the red (oldest) block is removed automatically from the board.
  8. You can press the Reset Game button at any time to start a fresh match.
  9. Feeling confident? Tap Try Quad Strike to play the larger and more challenging 5x5 version!
